Overnight Oats

Good day to you, reader, I thank you for coming across this recipe page. Below you will find a simple yet filling recipe for a healthy breakfast that will power your day. Think of this as a blank slate for you to add your preferred fruit, granola, or other breakfast toppings.

  • Plain oats (steel cut, rolled, whatever you prefer)
  • Milk (cow, almond, oat, whatever you like)
  • Some sort of container
  • Fruits, granola, or any of your favorite toppings
Steps:
  1. Pour some oats into your container.
  2. Pour some milk into your container, enough to cover the oats by about a quarter of an inch.
  3. Cover your container. Put in the fridge the night before you plan to eat.
  4. When you are ready to enjoy, place into another bowl and top with your preferred toppings. Dig IN!
