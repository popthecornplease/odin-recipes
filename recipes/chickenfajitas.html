Chicken Fajitas

Yo what's good foodie family, thanks for checking out this recipe page for chicken fajitas. Chicken fajitas are an absolutely exquisite way to enjoy a balanced meal full of protein. All you need to do is go to your local market to purchase some pre seasoned chicken, fire up the oven, and you're in business. Use your favorite tortillas and salsa, sear some onions and bell peppers, and you'll be well on your way to chow town. See below for the recipe, and be sure to check back for more!

  • Pre seasoned pollo asado (I prefer thighs)
  • Tortillas (Uncooked for the best experience and mouthfeel)
  • 1 medium sweet onion
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 Bell pepper of your favorite color
Steps:
  1. Preheat oven to 425 F
  2. Lightly oil a baking sheet
  3. Place your chicken thighs on the baking sheet and then put the sheet in the oven. Cook for 30 minutes.
  4. Slice bell pepper, garlic, and onion lengthwise.
  5. Heat a pan to medium high heat. Add some oil.
  6. Add your vegetables to the pan, and cook until seared. Remove from pan.
  7. Cook tortillas to package directions.
  8. Serve with your favorite salsa.
